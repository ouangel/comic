FREDERIC BOILET

MAKIKO PARADE

She read Yukiko's spinach for the hundreth time. Was that really her looking so young? In the four years since then she had been the mangaka's only model and love. This trip to the beautiful island of Enoshima was typical. They would find the best shooting locations, of course, but also enjoy each other again.
It is the rainy season and the hydrangeas are in full bloom. He knew he would leave soon...

YUKIKO'S SPINACH

Narrator or character? Reality or fiction? In this tender work of cinema, Boilet recounts an intimate love story in today's Tokyo between an everyday japanese girl, Yukiko, and her occidental lover. Passionate, gentle, fresh and vibrant. A love so strong it hurts as the story unfolds in a host of innovative techniques both narrative and visual. Very French... very Japanese... very Nouvelle Manga.

TOKYO IS MY GARDEN

When David Martin is sent as a sales representative to Japan for Heurault cognacs of France, he spends his time discovering Japanese culture and finding his way through the night-life of Tokyo. Then his boss announces an imminent visit to check up on his (lack of) progress. He must turn the situation around quickly or risk losing his job, his visa and the woman he loves. By Jiro Taniguchi and Frederic Boilet.
