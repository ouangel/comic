LIEUTENANT BLUEBERRY

After the Civil War, U. S. Cavalry Lt. Mike Blueberry wanders the American Southwest getting into and out of trouble, but mostly in. One of the great series in all of comics! Back in the late 60's, Jean Giraud, known as Moebius, one of France's greatest comic artists, collaborated with Jean-Michel Charlier on this series of stories of the American West.

AIRTIGHT GARAGE (Vol. I-IV)

The world of Le Garage Hermétique is governed by a semi-god like figure called Major Grubert. He takes on different physical attributes during the story, but is mostly seen as a mustachioed man wearing an old colonial uniform. Pointy helmet and all. Grubert’s labyrinthine world is composed of three levels: the unconscious, the conscious and super conscious.

(per volume) $30
