OUR STORY

Comics’ FiFi opened in 1997, and has become the leading comic book retailer in Vancouver, Canada with its online store. Comic’s FiFi offers the best independent or alternative Japanese and French comic selections, such as Taniguchi, Miyazaki, Tezuka, Moebius, and Boilet.

The best online comic book subscription service is also found at Comic's FiFi online store, with no deposit and no commitment.

From Vancouver to your door! Comic’s FiFi is committed to providing great service and prices, and making the shopping experience as much fun as reading and collecting.
