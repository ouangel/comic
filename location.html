Comic's FiFi is located on 1470 Commercial Dr, only one block away from the Commercial- Broadway station. Commercial Drive, or "The Drive", is one of Vancouver’s oldest and best-known business districts, and the multi-generational small businesses that make up the dynamic area help form the fabric of our city. Comic's FiFi is definitely one of these shops that will make you stop and walk-in. Our interesting selection of the best japanese and french authors will give you hours and hours of reading pleasure.
